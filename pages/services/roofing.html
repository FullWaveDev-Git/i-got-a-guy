Roofing in Loveland & Greater Cincinnati

Your roof is your home's first line of defense against the elements. Whether you need a quick repair, a full replacement, or just want a professional to take a look, I Got A Guy delivers dependable roofing services you can trust.

Roof Repairs

From missing shingles to small leaks, we handle roof repairs quickly and effectively. Catching problems early prevents costly damage down the road, so don't wait to address even minor issues.

Roof Replacements

When repairs aren't enough, a full roof replacement may be the best investment for your home. We work with quality materials and provide thorough installation to ensure your new roof stands up to years of Ohio weather.

Roof Inspections

Not sure about the condition of your roof? We offer thorough roof inspections to identify potential issues before they become expensive problems. Perfect for homebuyers, seasonal checks, or after major storms.

Storm Damage

High winds, hail, and heavy rain can wreak havoc on your roof. If your home has been impacted by a storm, we'll assess the damage and work with you to restore your roof as quickly as possible.

Signs Your Roof May Need Attention

Watch for missing or curling shingles, water stains on ceilings, granules in gutters, sagging roof sections, or daylight visible through the attic. If you notice any of these, give us a call for a free inspection.
