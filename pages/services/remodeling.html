Remodeling in Loveland & Greater Cincinnati

Whether you're looking to update a single room or overhaul your entire home, I Got A Guy delivers professional remodeling services designed around your lifestyle and budget. We work closely with you from start to finish, ensuring every detail meets your expectations.

Kitchen Remodeling

The kitchen is the heart of the home, and we treat it that way. Our kitchen remodeling services include cabinet installation, countertop upgrades, backsplash tiling, lighting updates, and full layout redesigns. Whether you want a modern open-concept kitchen or a classic, cozy space, we'll make it happen.

Bathroom Remodeling

From outdated tile to cramped layouts, we handle every aspect of bathroom renovations. Our services include shower and tub installations, vanity upgrades, tile work, plumbing fixture replacements, and complete bathroom redesigns that maximize both style and function.

Basement Finishing & Remodeling

Turn your unfinished basement into valuable living space. We handle framing, drywall, flooring, lighting, and all the finishing touches to create a space perfect for entertaining, working from home, or adding an extra bedroom.

Whole-Home Renovations

Planning a major transformation? We coordinate multi-room renovations to ensure consistency in design and quality throughout your home. From structural changes to cosmetic upgrades, we manage every detail so you don't have to.

What to Expect When Working With Us

Every remodeling project starts with a free consultation. We'll visit your home, discuss your ideas, and provide a detailed estimate. Once we begin, you'll have clear communication at every stage, from demolition through final walkthrough. We respect your home, keep the workspace clean, and stand behind our work.
