Deck Services

Extend your living space outdoors with a custom-built deck, or restore your existing deck to its former glory. We handle everything from new construction to repairs and refinishing.

Free Estimate

Decks in Loveland & Greater Cincinnati

A well-built deck adds value, beauty, and functional outdoor living space to your home. Whether you're dreaming of a brand-new deck or need to repair and refinish an existing one, I Got A Guy has the experience to get it done right.

Custom Deck Building

We design and build custom decks tailored to your home's layout and your lifestyle. From simple platform decks to multi-level designs with built-in seating and railings, we use quality materials and proven techniques for a deck that lasts.

Deck Repairs

Loose boards, wobbly railings, rotting joists, or damaged stairs? We diagnose the issue and make targeted repairs to restore your deck's safety and appearance without replacing the entire structure.

Deck Refinishing & Staining

Over time, weather and foot traffic take their toll. Our refinishing services include power washing, sanding, staining, and sealing to protect your deck and bring back its natural beauty.

Permits and Code Compliance

Building or modifying a deck often requires permits and must meet local building codes. We handle the permitting process and ensure every project is built to code for your safety and peace of mind.

What to Expect

We start with a free on-site consultation to discuss your vision, assess your yard, and provide a detailed estimate. Throughout the build, we maintain a clean workspace, communicate progress, and ensure the finished product exceeds your expectations.
